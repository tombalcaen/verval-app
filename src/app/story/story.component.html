Thank you

First of all, thank you for checking out this 'waste less' app. This means that you might want to support us in our mission to create a more conscious world towards food waste.

The reason we are so motivated is because even in the 21 century there are still people having a hard time finding qualitative and affordable food and we are not only talking about the people in underdeveloped countries.

How can you support us even more?

  • Sign up to our monthly newsletter.

    Every month we send a newsletter regarding the new inititives, updates and community works of fellow innovators and social heroes.

  • Give us constructive feedback

    Let us know how we can improve our service and help the world towards a better, waste less future.

  • Spread the word

    Share our message on your favorite social media outlet or talk about us the old fashioned way.

  • Donate

    You can help us keeping this initiative alive by donating. 50% of all donations go to feeding the less fortunate and other support organisations in our local and not so local communities.
